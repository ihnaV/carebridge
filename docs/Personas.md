# Personas

## Primary Persona: Caregiver
A caregiver manages day-to-day support for a loved one and needs a simple way to organize care tasks, reminders, and communication.

## Secondary Persona: Patient
A patient wants to stay informed about appointments, medications, and support resources without confusion.

## Tertiary Persona: Provider
A provider or clinical team needs visibility into care coordination progress and relevant follow-up information.

## Persona Needs
- Clear, easy-to-use workflows.
- Reliable reminders and updates.
- Secure access to relevant information.
