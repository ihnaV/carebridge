# Vision

## Purpose
CareBridge aims to simplify care coordination for patients, caregivers, and providers by providing a centralized, secure platform for communication, scheduling, and follow-up.

## Goals
- Improve communication between caregivers and families.
- Make care tasks and appointments easier to track.
- Provide a clear, reliable view of care progress.

## Success Criteria
- Users can quickly access key care information.
- Care teams can coordinate tasks with less friction.
- The platform supports future growth and additional services.
